

### 🛡️ The 'Stay Safe' Checklist

* **UPI PIN Rule:** Remember, you **never** enter your PIN to receive money. If someone asks you to "scan to receive," it is a scam.
* **Verify "Digital Arrests":** Official agencies (CBI/Police) will never arrest you via a WhatsApp video call. Hang up immediately if threatened.
* **The ₹1 Test:** Before paying a new PG owner or seller, send a test transaction of ₹1 to verify the recipient’s identity.
* **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Enable 2FA on WhatsApp and Instagram to prevent account hijacking.
* **Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Banking:** Use mobile data for financial transactions; public networks are easily intercepted.
* **Employment Scams:** Reject "Like and Earn" jobs on Telegram. If they ask for an "upfront fee," it’s a fraud.
* **Post-Dated Posting:** Avoid live-tagging your location. Post your "campus hangs" after you’ve left the spot.
* **Update Software:** Those OS updates contain critical security patches—never skip them.

### 🚨 Reporting Channels
If defrauded, act within the "Golden Hour." Call **1930** or report online at **cybercrime.gov.in**.
