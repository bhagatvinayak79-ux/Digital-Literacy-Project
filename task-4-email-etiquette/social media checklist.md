


### Social Media Checklist

| **Do's** | **Don'ts** |

| **Audit your privacy settings** regularly to control who can see your personal posts and tagged photos. | **Don't post impulsive rants** or emotionally charged content regarding professors, peers, or employers. |
| **Network intentionally** by connecting with alumni, industry professionals, and campus organizations on LinkedIn. | **Don't share sensitive information**, such as your student ID number, residential address, or current exact location. |
| **Showcase your projects** and academic achievements to build a positive "digital portfolio" for recruiters. | **Don't engage with "trolls"** or participate in online bullying/harassment, as these digital trails are permanent. |
| **Verify information** before sharing news or articles to avoid spreading misinformation or "fake news." | **Don't post content depicting illegal acts** or policy violations that could jeopardize your standing with the university. |
| **Use a professional profile picture** and a clean bio on platforms where you might interact with recruiters. | **Don't forget that "private" is never 100% private**; assume anything you post could be screenshotted and shared. |
